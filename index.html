Carbon Equality is a global initiative for carbon markets transformation on principles of transparency and equality.

We bring together those who reduce GHG emissions in the most effective manner and those who need to offset own, or their product carbon footprint, in a simple way

The Initiative will Change the Spirit of the Planet.

Carbon equality initiative (Initiative) - a broad based social movement for a radical Reformation of the climate movement, changing the principles of the carbon market, a steady transition from the baseline 2021 to low emissions target year of 2050, and the elimination of bureaucratic obstacles along the way

Climate movement

Combat with global climate change has been going on for more than 30 years. Despite numerous conferences, efforts taken by some countries and large companies, serious results are yet to be reached. GHG content in the air increases by 0,5-0,7 % yearly. Total emissions increase by 350-500 million tons  every year   with the population growth. Per capita emissions of 6,6 thousand tons per year do not decrease.Many climate activists and the general public gather the  efforts are futile. But this situation can be changed.

By discarding the false concept of “net zero”,  reducing emissions by about 20 billion tons of CO2e per year, we can stabilize the GHG content in the Earth’s atmosphere and maintain existing climatic conditions, adaptation to which is not difficult. Thus, the problem of global climate change on Earth can be solved

A radical reform of the climate movement is needed

Emission and
carbon footprint
assessment
We enable the assessment of the carbon footprint:
  • for legal entities using service of professional organizations
  • after verification (re-verification), the assessments of emissions and carbon footprint obtained by emitters earlier can be taken into account.

The Initiative, at the request of the Client, with the participation of partner professional organizations (PPOs), can also provide the following assessment services

  • the mass of the issuer's emissions for a certain period
  • the emitter’'s carbon footprint for the year or since the beginning of the base period (2021)
  • manufactured products carbon footprint
  • cumulative annual increase in emissions for the period since base year of 2021 to year preceding current one
  • results of de-emission measures in tons of CO2e, assistance in registering and obtaining DTD and CU
