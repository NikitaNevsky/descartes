Care about your impact?

Correct and transparent assessment of carbon units is the basis of the Initiative.

This assessment is based on taking into account the accumulated annual increase compared to the base level for the period from the base (2021) year to the year preceding the current one, and is necessary to assess the effectiveness of measures to de-emit GHG, obtain certificates in the NFT format, confirming the emitter’s contribution to global climate change fight.

In the event that a GHG emitter offsets the accumulated increase in emissions compared to the base year, the defined tons of de-emissions (DTD) received during its activities can be recognized as carbon units and used to offset the carbon footprint of other emitters and become the basis for minting digital carbon units DCRT.

Solving the global problems of climate change requires climate mobilization and the involvement of as many GHG emitters as possible, ideally the entire population of the planet.
